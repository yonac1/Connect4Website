Rules Of The Game

Each player has one-color discs, and each player in turn inserts a disc inserted into the board which consists of seven columns and six rows.

How To Win

All you need to do in this game is to connect 4 circles of the same color. You can connect in vertical, balanced, or diagonal sequence.

The Best Out Of #3

One game consists of 3 rounds. The game will end when the 3 rounds are over, the winner of the game will be the best out of the three rounds - the leading player in the final score.
